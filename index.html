About me

Hello, There!

I'm Pankaj, and I'm passionate about mobile app development. With a deep understanding of Flutter and a solid foundation in React Native, I enjoy crafting elegant and efficient solutions for cross-platform mobile applications.

My journey into the world of mobile app development began when I discovered Flutter, and I instantly fell in love with its flexibility and rich ecosystem. Over the years, I've honed my skills in Flutter, exploring its various features, widgets, and plugins. I have hands-on experience in building beautiful and performant applications using Flutter's reactive framework. Whether it's creating stunning user interfaces or integrating complex functionalities, I thrive on the challenges that Flutter brings to the table.

While Flutter is my primary expertise, I also have a good grasp of React Native. I've explored React Native's component-based architecture and leveraged its powerful libraries to build applications that target both iOS and Android platforms. Though my focus has been predominantly on Flutter, my exposure to React Native has given me a broader perspective and an appreciation for different approaches to mobile development.
